Panel Bender

Automatic loading & unloading system

  • Apply to medium-to-thin sheets and medium-to-thick sheets
  • Unmanned and full automatic operation
  • One-to-many work

Features

Automatic

Sheet metals are automatically loaded to exchange platforms and finished products are stacked to pallets to minimize work intensity and save labour costs.

Efficient

The loading is highly efficient to greatly improve cutting efficiency, save non-productive duration and lower energy consumption.

Safe

With digital operation and safe handling, sheet metals are well protected from scratching and finished products will be at higher quality.

Intelligent Layer-based Measurement of Sheet Thickness

Sheet metals are detected intelligently, and only one sheet will be loaded at a time. The sheet is subject to accurate measurement of thickness and put on different pallets.

One-to-many Operation by Modular Extension

A set of automatic loading & unloading system can simultaneously serve for several cutting machines to make efficient production possible. Except for reduction in energy consumption, the utilization of workshop will also be enhanced.
