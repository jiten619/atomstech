PU Gasket Dispensing Machine

Compact & easy-to-use gasket dispensing machine

  • Max laser source 12W
  • 11.8” X 8.2” working area
  • Closed-loop DC servo control
  • Automatic Z-axis movement

Features

Drag-N-Play

An exceptionally innovative and user-friendly feature based on servomotor technology simplifies operations and shortens setup time. Simply hand drag the lens carriage of PU gasket dispensing machine to the desired position to be engraved and press the start button to commence the job.

AutoFocus (Patented)

Patented Auto Focus Gauge adjusts PU gasket dispensing machine to a precise focal point on the engraving area automatically. The Auto Focus is the most effective lens-focusing tool that prevents human error when exact measurement of the distance from the surface of the working material is needed.

Unique Feature for Making Stamps

The 2" diameter hole is provided for rubber stamps with a longer body. The size of this pit fits your stamp sample perfectly. Just position the stamp stick into the hole of PU gasket dispensing machine, the longer stamp can be made with ease.

User-Friendly Interface

GCC LaserPro windows-based driver provides powerful function settings, such as laser engraving mode, programmable origin, various dithering types, and more to meet users' specific needs.

Special Fire Retardant Material

The flexible right and left table shields are designed to coordinate the up-down Z rail. These two shields isolate the airflow in the middle of the working table to reduce the possible damage caused by the heat during laser engraving.

RoHS Compliance

All PU gasket dispensing machines are RoHS-compliant (the European Parliament Directive 2002/ 95/ EC, Restriction of Hazardous Substances) and lead-free (pb free). PU gasket will continue to concern and provide eco-friendly products to protect the earth for our further generations.

Windows-based Driver

Windows-based drivers offer plenty of functionality, including pitch tuning to adjust for a vertical profile for each character during stamp laser engraving.

Precise Closed-loop DC Servo Motor

The PU gasket dispensing machine utilizes high-speed Closed-loop DC servo motors to shorten the overall laser engraving time while maintaining excellent process quality.

Unique Design Down Vacuum Table

A4 size working table and the unique dust collecting mechanism allows the full table rubber stamp to be engraved and debris to be gathered easily all at once.

Built-In Networking Connection

Network connection enables multiple laser engravers to be operated by one PC and multiple PCs to share a single unit of laser engraver. The Ethernet port has made data transmission easier and faster for GCC LaserPro users using network connection. Free your designers with great flexibility and maximum machine versatility.
